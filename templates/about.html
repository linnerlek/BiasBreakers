BiasBreakers

Our Mission

Our platform immerses users in challenging workplace scenarios, with a particular focus on addressing internalized sexism and sexual harassment. Through interactive decision-making and playful yet practical feedback, we empower users to actively challenge gender bias, become better allies, and foster a workplace culture that’s fair, supportive, and inclusive for everyone. Our goal is to equip users with the skills to handle tough situations and create lasting change—all while keeping things engaging and real.
