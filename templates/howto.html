How to Play

Navigate realistic workplace scenarios related to bias, harassment, and allyship. Make decisions that shape outcomes, learn from feedback, and improve your skills in creating a respectful workplace.

Step 1: Read the scenario

Review different situations, considering emotions, power dynamics, and potential risks.

Step 2: Make a Decisions

Choose the best response from multiple options to promote an inclusive environment.

Step 3: Receive Feedback

Learn from positive outcomes, challenges, and advice to enhance your real-world handling of similar situations.

Step 4: Replay and Reflect

Explore different choices to understand their impact and apply insights to improve workplace culture.
