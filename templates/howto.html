How to Play

Overview

Em-POW-her immerses players in real-world workplace scenarios. Through interactive decisions, you can practice handling situations involving bias, harassment, and advocacy in a safe environment.

Step 1: Choose Your Role

Select to play as an Employee, Bystander, or Manager. Each perspective provides unique challenges and learning opportunities:

  • Employee: Experience the challenges of confronting bias or reporting harassment.
  • Bystander: Decide how to support colleagues witnessing inappropriate behavior.
  • Manager: Lead by handling complaints and fostering an inclusive workplace.

Step 2: Make Decisions

Each scenario offers multiple-choice responses. Select the response you feel is best and see how it impacts the situation.
